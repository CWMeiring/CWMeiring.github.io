About Me

I am a creative entrepreneur with a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision, bridging the worlds of technology and craftsmanship. My journey began with a solid foundation in computer science through A+, followed by continuous self-directed learning in web development and digital technologies.

This technical background has been instrumental in shaping my approach to creative entrepreneurship. Where others see only art, I see systems, processes, and opportunities for innovation. My early technical training gave me the analytical skills to approach complex problems methodically, while my passion for technology keeps me at the forefront of digital fabrication tools and techniques.

Today, I channel this technical mindset into my creative ventures. At Bangarang Crafts, I combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology, using 3D printing, digital design tools, and e-commerce platforms to create unique, fandom-inspired pieces. At Hearth & Forge Studios, I leverage my technical skills to bring fantasy worlds to life through precision digital modeling and fabrication.

My mission is to demonstrate that technical skills and creative expression aren't separate domains—they're complementary forces that, when combined, enable truly innovative work. Whether I'm writing Python scripts to automate business processes, using AI-assisted tools to enhance my design workflow, or troubleshooting a complex 3D print, I'm applying the same problem-solving mindset I developed through my technical education.

I believe that the future belongs to those who can bridge disciplines, and I'm passionate about showing how technical knowledge can elevate creative work and how creative thinking can solve technical challenges.
